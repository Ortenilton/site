Ortenilton S. Filho

Ph.D. student in Mathematics @ University of Coimbra

SHORT BIO

Ortenilton has a degree in Mathematics from the Federal University of Sergipe (Brasil, 2021) and a master's degree in Mathematics from the Pontifical Catholic University of Rio de Janeiro (Brasil, 2023). As an undergraduate, he participated three times in the Institutional Program for Scientific Initiation Scholarships (PIBIC), as well as the Institutional Program for Teaching Initiation Scholarships (PIBID). As a master's student, he was funded by a high-level selection scheme, the FAPERJ Mestrado Nota 10 scholarship.

He is currently a Ph.D. student in Mathematics at the University of Coimbra, under the joint supervision of Edgard Pimentel and Ercília Sousa. His research interests focus on the analysis of partial differential equations, more specifically, on exploring results at the core of the theory of viscosity solutions. Recently, he has directed his efforts to the study of fractional vanishing viscosity.
